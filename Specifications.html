Specifications

We use various parts to make drone flying and landing or takeoff.

Wings:

The Talon UAV features fixed wings that provide lift and stability during flight. The wingspan can vary depending on the specific model and payload requirements.

Propulsion System:

The Talon UAV is typically equipped with an electric motor or a small gasoline engine that drives the propeller(s). The propulsion system generates thrust for the UAV's forward motion and controls its speed and altitude.

Battery:

Depending on the specific model, the Talon UAV can be powered by a battery pack for the propulsion system. Batteries provide electrical power for electric motor-driven UAVs.

Avionics:

The avionics system includes flight control systems, autopilot, navigation systems, and communication equipment. These components provide control and navigation capabilities, ensuring safe and autonomous flight operations.
